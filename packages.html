Subscription FAQs

Is there a contract?

No, there are no contracts. You will be charged monthly.

Can I get additional email volume?

Yes, you can get monthly or annual email volume bundles. Please contact our Sales team to get pricing relevant to your needs.

What is the best package for my business?

Makesbridge offers 5 different packages that have been designed after studying needs of different business models and sizes. The best way to pick up the right package is to cross-compare features and your unique business needs. In addition, with our cost calculator, you can customize your package as per your unique marketing needs. Check features comparison page to compare our packages.

Is there any startup cost?

No, there is no startup cost. We offer free deployment and support services.

What is the Concierge Service?

Our Concierge Service provides hands-on administrative support. Our qualified staff takes over the tasks of running your campaigns. So, you don't have to spend thousands of dollars’ worth of man-hours of your marketing team doing mundane tasks like; optimizing emails, testing email templates on different email clients, deliverability, lists management, segmentation, setting up nurture tracks and workflows, and many other time consuming, routine activities.

How do I get support?

Our support team is available between 9:00 - 5:00 Pacific Time. You can reach us out via chat, email and phone.

What is the cancellation policy?

We have a 30-day cancellation policy. Please read about the cancellation policy in our 'Terms of service'.

Can I compare Makesbridge with mainstream marketing automation platforms?

Makesbridge is for everyone. We have users who send from a few hundred to millions of emails every month. There are customers who do simple blast emailing and also those who have complex requirements and the most sophisticated marketing automation scenarios. We feel very happy to claim that Makesbridge can handle any kind of business needs with regards to email marketing, automation, sales or analytics. Check cross-comparison of Makesbridge with MailChimp, ConstantConctact, HubSpot, and Marketo on our cost calculator page.

Is there a discount for non-profit organizations?

Yes, Makesbridge offers discounted pricing for non-profit organizations. To seek specific information about such pricing, please contact our support.

Can I subscribe for a year, instead of month?

Yes, if you do not have a fixed monthly email volume, it would be ideal for you to subscribe for a year. This way, you can get annual email volume and additional discount on the gross pricing.

Do you provide CRM (Salesforce, NetSuite), marketing or sales related consulting services?

We have a partner network of certified CRM consultants who specialize in all areas of CRM consultancy. They can help you get the most out of your CRM, whether it is Salesforce or any other CRM platform. In addition, we also have partnership with several management consultants with decent expertise in marketing, sales and other areas of business. Makesbridge has a dedicated consultancy offer. If you subscribe for MKS Pro or higher, we will pay on your behalf for 5 hours of consultancy. Please contact us to find out more about Makesbridge free consultancy offers.

Integrate marketing capabilities and results data into popular CRM apps in minutes. Data is always synched for hassle free targeting and automatic activity updates to contact records.
